💳 Billing & Payments

My payment was deducted but plan not activated
Email [email protected] with your payment screenshot and order ID. We resolve these within 2-4 hours.
How do I get a refund?
We offer a 3-day refund for first-time subscribers. Email [email protected]. See our Refund Policy for full details.
What payment methods are accepted?
UPI, Net Banking, Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), and digital wallets — via Cashfree.
Does my plan auto-renew?
No. Plans do not auto-renew. You must manually renew before expiry. We send email reminders 7 and 2 days before expiry.

👤 Account & Access

I forgot my password
Go to Login page, enter your email, and click "Forgot password?" — a reset link will be sent to your inbox.
Can I use the same account on multiple devices?
Yes, your account can be logged in on multiple devices simultaneously.
How do I delete my account?
Email [email protected] with the subject "Delete Account". We process deletion within 7 business days.

⚙️ Using the Engines

Which engines are free?
Risk Profiler and Market News are completely free. Goal Planner and Retirement require Basic+. Stock Analysis, Portfolio, and Global Events require Pro.
Is the analysis personalized investment advice?
No. All analysis is for educational purposes. We are not SEBI registered. Always consult a qualified financial advisor before making investment decisions.
How current is the market data?
Market news and stock data is refreshed every 15 minutes during market hours (9:15 AM – 3:30 PM IST, Mon–Fri).
