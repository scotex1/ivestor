Your information is used to provide and improve FinVest Pro services, send transactional emails (plan confirmations, expiry notices), and personalize your dashboard experience. We do not use your data for unsolicited marketing without consent.

Your data is stored securely on Firebase (Google Cloud) and our backend servers. Authentication is handled by Firebase Auth with industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect against unauthorized access.
